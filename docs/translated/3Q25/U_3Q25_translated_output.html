# Unity Q3 Earnings Call Summary

## Key Financial Highlights
- **Grow segment revenue accelerated 11% sequentially in Q3**, driven by Vector AI's improved performance
- **Create segment growth** driven by three factors: price increases rolling through P&L, China expansion (15% to 20% of total revenue), and industry business growth
- **EBITDA margins improved 200 bps YoY and 400 bps from Q1**, with gross margins at 82-83%
- **Cash increased ~$500M over past year**, primarily from strong free cash flow conversion
- **Q4 Grow guidance: mid-single digit sequential growth** (deceleration from Q3's 11%)

## Strategic Initiatives & Product Updates
- **Vector AI** showing broad-based strength across all geographies, platforms, and game genres; management emphasizes sustainable long-term growth trajectory independent of runtime data
- **Developer Data Framework** launched in August with strong opt-in rates; positioned as multi-year growth opportunity rather than near-term catalyst
- **Unity in-app payments** launched as free service to help developers navigate opening app store ecosystem; modest direct revenue but strategic platform expansion
- **China momentum** particularly strong in gaming and automotive/industrial applications; Unity is only platform compatible with all Chinese platforms including Open Harmony
