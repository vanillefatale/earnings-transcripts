# Align Technology Q3 2025 Earnings Call Summary

## Key Financial Metrics & Performance
- **Clear aligner volume growth**: 9 out of top 10 markets showed year-over-year improvement from Q2 to Q3 (only Canada declined)
- **Teen case growth**: Up 8% year-over-year in Q3, consistently outpacing adult growth
- **Doctor reach**: Record 88,000 doctors served (over 90% have iTero scanners)
- **Scanner installed base**: Over 100,000 units globally
- **UK VAT benefit**: ~2/3 benefit realized in Q3, full quarter impact expected in Q4 (~20% price increase effective August)

## Strategic Developments & Guidance
- **Growth drivers**: Strong international performance (EMEA, APAC, Latin America) offsetting softer US demand
- **Product portfolio expansion**: New launches include Palatal Expander, mandibular advancement with occlusal blocks; "3 and 3" product (3-year treatment, 3 refinements) now best-selling SKU at lower ASP but higher gross margin rate
- **Go-to-market shift**: Focusing on "active conversion" doctors (DSOs, high-volume practices using local marketing, patient financing, scanning protocols) seeing double-digit growth vs. "passive" practices struggling
- **Target range maintained**: 5-15% volume/revenue growth guidance unchanged
