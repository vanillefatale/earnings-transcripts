# HCA Healthcare Q4 2025 Earnings Call Summary

## Key Financial Metrics & Guidance

- **2026 Revenue & EBITDA Guidance**: Midpoint suggests stable margins versus 2025, maintaining ~20% EBITDA margin despite significant headwinds
- **Exchange Impact**: Estimated $600-900M adverse EBITDA impact from ACA subsidy expiration and administrative reforms
- Modeling 15-20% decline in exchange volumes (8% of admissions, 10% of revenue in 2025)
- 15-20% of lost exchange patients expected to migrate to employer-sponsored insurance; remainder to uninsured
- 30% utilization decline assumed for those becoming uninsured
- **Supplemental Payments**: $250-450M net benefit decline expected (includes Texas ATLIS program pause, Virginia retro payment timing, Tennessee program reduction)
- **Resiliency Program**: $400M incremental cost savings targeted for 2026, focused on revenue integrity, cost efficiencies, and capacity management
- **Volume Guidance**: 2-3% growth expected despite exchange headwinds
- **Capital Expenditure**: $5.0-5.5B planned for 2026, with ~$7B in approved pipeline projects

## Management Tone

**Confident and proactive** despite policy uncertainties. Management emphasized:
- Strong execution capability on cost management initiatives
- Resiliency program as cultural imperative, not one-time event
- Confidence in maintaining long-term margin targets through operational leverage and digital transformation
- Pleased with Q4 performance: outpatient revenue growth exceeded inpatient, strong payer mix, effective expense management

## Key Strengths

- **Operational Excellence**: Contract labor stabilized at 4.2% of salaries/wages/benefits; working capital improved with reduced net days in AR
- **Digital Transformation**: Active payer partnerships driving administrative simplification, electronic data exchange, and dispute resolution improvements
- **Diversified Portfolio**: No division >10% of profits; no service line >15% of revenue
- **Outpatient Expansion**: Added ~100 outpatient facilities in 2025; targeting 18-20 outpatient
