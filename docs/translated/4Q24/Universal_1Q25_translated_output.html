📌 요약

Universal Corporation reported a strong start to fiscal year 2025 in their Q1 earnings call on August 7, 2024. The company's revenue increased by 15% to $597.1 million, with significant growth in both the Tobacco and Ingredients Operations segments. Operating income rose by 56% to $17.2 million compared to the same quarter last year. The growth in the Tobacco segment was attributed to higher sales volumes and prices, while the Ingredients segment benefited from increased sales volume and new product sales. Net income for the quarter was $0.1 million, or $0.01 per diluted share, showing an improvement from the previous year. The company also highlighted its commitment to sustainability, having received third-party verification of its greenhouse gas emissions data.
