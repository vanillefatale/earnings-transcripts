📌 요약

During a Q&A session, Ann Hynes inquired about the factors influencing the 2025 MLR guidance. Celeste Mellet responded by outlining the primary drivers for changes in the MLR. Decreases in the MLR are mainly due to exits from MA plans with high benefit ratios, adjustments in remaining plans, and a favorable 2025 calendar influenced by leap year adjustments. On the other hand, increases in the MLR are attributed to a growing Medicaid business with higher benefit ratios, impacts from the IRA which increases revenue but also claims, and incremental investments that are expected to raise the benefit ratio in the short term but are beneficial long-term.
