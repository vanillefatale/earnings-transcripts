During a financial Q&A session, Celeste Mellet responded to Ann Hynes' inquiry about the 2025 MLR guidance. Mellet explained that the decrease in the MLR ratio is primarily due to exits from MA plans with high benefit ratios and adjustments in remaining plans, along with a favorable 2025 calendar. Conversely, the increase in the MLR ratio is attributed to a growing Medicaid business mix, impacts from the IRA, and incremental investments, all of which temporarily raise the benefit ratio.
